Find the nth Fibonacci Series Element.

The Fibonacci Series: 
Starts with two numbers, 1 and 1

The next element of the series is the sum of the previous two elements.
For example:
The third element of the Fibonacci Series is the sum of the Second and First Elements.
3rd Fibonacci Element = 2nd Fibonacci Element + 1st Fibonacci Element
3rd Fibonacci Element = 1 + 1
3rd Fibonacci Element = 2


The series goes on like:
1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13, 21...
